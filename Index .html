The Panel

Five tests. One draw. A complete internal picture.

Every test in this package was selected by our clinical team to address the most prevalent and actionable health risks for adults in their 30s, 40s, and 50s.

01

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Evaluates red blood cells, white blood cells, hemoglobin, hematocrit, and platelets. Screens for anemia, infection, clotting disorders, and immune system conditions.

02

Blood Glucose (Fasting)

Measures circulating blood sugar levels to screen for pre-diabetes and type 2 diabetes. Early detection enables dietary and pharmacological intervention before complications develop.

03

Lipid Profile

Quantifies total cholesterol, LDL ("bad"), HDL ("good"), and triglycerides — the primary cardiovascular risk markers guiding treatment decisions for heart disease prevention.

04

Liver Function Tests (LFT)

Measures enzymes and proteins (ALT, AST, ALP, bilirubin, albumin) to assess liver health. Identifies fatty liver disease, medication effects, and hepatic disorders at an early, treatable stage.

05

Kidney Function Tests (KFT / RFT)

Evaluates creatinine, urea, uric acid, and electrolytes to assess how efficiently the kidneys are filtering waste. Essential for those with hypertension, diabetes risk, or family history of renal disease.

FAQ

Answers before you book.

Do I need to fast before the blood draw? +

Yes — a 10–12 hour fast is required before your appointment. This ensures accurate fasting blood glucose and triglyceride readings. You may drink plain water. We'll send a full preparation checklist after you book.

How long does the blood draw take? +

The actual blood collection takes approximately 5–10 minutes. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early to complete a brief intake form. Most patients are in and out within 20 minutes.

When will I receive my results? +

Processed results are delivered digitally within 24–48 hours of sample collection. You'll receive an email notification with a secure link to access and download your report.

Are these results shared with my primary care physician? +

Not automatically. Your results are private and HIPAA-protected. You can choose to share them with any provider you wish. We provide a shareable PDF formatted to standard medical records conventions.

What if my results show an abnormal value? +

All reports are reviewed by a licensed physician. If any value falls outside clinically normal ranges, a nurse will contact you within 24 hours to explain the finding and recommend appropriate next steps. Abnormal results do not necessarily mean disease — context always matters, and our team is trained to help you understand yours.

Is this package suitable if I have a known condition like diabetes or hypertension? +

Yes. This panel is beneficial both for early detection in healthy adults and for ongoing monitoring in those with existing conditions. If you are currently under a physician's care, we recommend sharing your results with them for integrated management.

Is home sample collection available? +

Yes, home collection is available in most service areas for a small additional fee. A certified phlebotomist will visit at your chosen time. Select the home visit option when booking.
